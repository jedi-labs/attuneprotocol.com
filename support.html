Common questions

Quick answers.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Attune → Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used to originally subscribe.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Attune Protocol → Cancel. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.

I lost my journal data. Can it be recovered?

Attune stores everything locally on your device with no cloud backup. If you've deleted the app or reset your phone without a device backup, the data cannot be recovered. We're sorry — this is the tradeoff of privacy-first, local-only storage.

Will there be an Android version?

Not at launch. V1 is iOS only. Once the iOS app covers its costs, Android is next.

Something in the app is broken. How do I report it?

Email attune@jedilabs.io with a description and your iOS version. Screenshots help. We'll get back to you fast.
