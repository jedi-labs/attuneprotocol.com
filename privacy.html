Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MAY 2026

Short version: Attune Protocol collects no personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to identify you.

What we collect

Nothing. Attune Protocol does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

All data you enter in the app — stress scores, journal entries, tomorrow's tasks, session history — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. It never leaves your phone.

Accounts

Attune Protocol requires no account, no email address, and no sign-in of any kind. There is no user profile, no username, and no way for us to identify you.

Analytics and tracking

We do not use analytics tools (Mixpanel, Amplitude, Firebase Analytics, etc.), advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels. We do not track your behavior inside the app.

The website (attuneprotocol.com) uses Google Analytics to measure page visits. This is limited to the website and does not extend to the app.

Subscriptions and payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to verify subscription status — this involves sharing an anonymous device identifier with RevenueCat's servers to confirm whether a subscription is active. No personal information is shared.

We never see your payment details. All billing is handled by Apple.

Crash reporting

We do not use third-party crash reporting tools. If the app crashes, we don't automatically receive a report. If you encounter a bug, please contact us at attune@jedilabs.io.

Your data

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Deleting the app permanently deletes all your data — there is no cloud backup to restore from.
  • We cannot access, retrieve, or delete your data on your behalf — we don't have it.
  • Pro users can export their journal as Markdown or PDF from within the app at any time.

Children

Attune Protocol is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Given that we collect no data, we don't anticipate significant changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email attune@jedilabs.io or join the Discord community.
